Our Solution

03 / 12

What if YouTube taught you a language?

Lingo Stream is a Chrome extension that swaps a few words in YouTube subtitles into your target language — right while you watch.

"The government (政府) announced a new policy (政策) to address (解决) the crisis (危机)."

No extra study time. No new app to open. Just watch what you already watch.

Smart Engine

05 / 12

Only the right words get swapped.

We don't just replace random words. Our processor picks high-value vocabulary that actually helps you learn.

  • Skips stop-words, numbers, and punctuation automatically
  • Keeps replacement rate at 5-15% so you still follow the video
  • Carries over missed words to the next subtitle line
  • Lets you pin words you want to see every time
🛡️

Speed
